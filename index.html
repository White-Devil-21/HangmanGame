HANGMAN GAME

The name of this game is Hangman. In this game you are asked to find the hidden word. You have maximum of 6 attempts to find that word. You can either click the letters from the virtual keyboard or from the physical keyboard if you are using a PC. If you find the word before the man hangs you win. For each right word you will get 10 points and for each wrong guess 2 points will be reduced from your points
